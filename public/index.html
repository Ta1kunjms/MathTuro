WORKFLOW

How The System Works

A simple 6-step workflow from content creation to progress tracking.

1

Teacher uploads learning materials

Modules, PDFs, videos, and quiz links are added to the system

2

Student studies lessons

Access all learning materials anytime, anywhere

3

Student takes quiz on external website

Complete assessments on integrated third-party platforms

4

Student uploads quiz screenshot + score

Submit proof of completion with results for verification

5

Teacher reviews and approves

Manual verification ensures academic integrity

6

Progress is updated automatically

Real-time tracking of student performance and completion
